Why does Web Academy exist?

The current path to getting a business online is broken. There is a knowledge gap in how to take a business idea and sucessfully execute it online. We bridge this gap through providing education, advice and know-how to startups and established small businesses. From planning to get online, to ongoing marketing strategies we aim to provide the topics that you need most.
